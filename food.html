We are a Uzbekistan cafe serving the best artisan coffee, dippy eggs, fresh sandwiches, cakes, homemade peanut butter, and lots of ❤ in Yashnobod.

We take pride in sourcing our ingredients directly from Region farmers. Our free range eggs are from a family run farm in Distrits and our meat is from rare breeds in Chirchiq. Bread is baked by artisan bakery the Flour station and cakes are made in our own kitchen by hand.
